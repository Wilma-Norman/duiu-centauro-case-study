The Story

What happened during the Drop

14 days. 8,200 creators. One Silver tier unlocked.

The Brief

Sallve came to DUIU with a clear tension: they had built a brand people genuinely loved, but the best evidence of that lived in private conversations, not in public. The Drop was called "Sua Pele, Sua Luz" — an open invitation for anyone to share their real skincare story, their routine, their results, with Sallve products or without. No filters required. No perfection expected. Just honesty.

What Happened

Within 48 hours the Drop had over 600 Replies. The content that performed best was not polished — it was a woman in Belo Horizonte filming her nighttime routine under bathroom light, no ring light, no script, just talking through each step. Her video collected 31,000 votes and became the emotional centrepiece of the Thread. The invite multiplier reached 1.8x as participants tagged friends who they thought "needed to see this." Sallve's marketing team spent the week reading comments instead of running ads.

The Outcome

The Silver prize tier unlocked on day ten, four days before the campaign closed. 1.8 million accounts reached, 38 percent returning to engage across multiple sessions. The Drop generated over 8,200 pieces of content that Sallve now uses across their own channels with full creator permission built into the platform. The cost to acquire that library was a fraction of a single traditional production shoot.

"We always knew people loved the product. After the Drop, we had 8,000 videos proving it — in their words, in their homes, in their own light."

Brand Director, Sallve — Post-campaign debrief
